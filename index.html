How to Debate

The Core Rules

  1. Clarity — State your position in one clean sentence.
  2. Evidence — Back claims with facts, data or examples.
  3. Logic — Build arguments with cause → effect → impact.
  4. Rebuttal — Address the opponent's strongest point directly.
  5. Delivery — Confident, structured, respectful tone.

Structure of a Round (PREP)

  • Point — What do you claim?
  • Reason — Why is it true?
  • Example — Show it in real life.
  • Point — Restate and drive it home.

JCI Spirit

Debate with respect. The goal is truth and growth, not humiliation. JCI members are Active Citizens — they listen, they learn, they lead.

Scoring Criteria

  • Argument strength (0–25)
  • Evidence & examples (0–25)
  • Rebuttal & engagement (0–25)
  • Delivery & structure (0–25)
